Tripod
Type Weapon device
Function Stationary heavy weapon platform

Composition

Ajatite 517.00

Bastium 827.30

Charodium 723.80

Tripod weapons are heavy infantry weaponry platforms that can hold different types of tripod weapons. While setting up a tripod takes a moment, tripod weapons can be easily taken off and swapped for another. Tripod weapons operate at a lowered fire rate when not attached to electricity. To attach a tripod weapon to electricity the operating endoskeleton needs to be connected to a resource bridge.
