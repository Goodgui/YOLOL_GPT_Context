Large generator side structure

Size 264×240×96 cm
Mass 21,517.23 kg
Volume 2,450.71 kv
Corrosion resistance 368
Primary material Ymrium
Input / Output

Heat capacity 1,500

Heat transfer 75 per second (to radiator)
Heat dissipation 5 per second

Sockets 4

Composition

Charodium 23%

Kutonium 10%
Lukium 17%

Valkite 10%

Ymrium 40%

Side structure for large generator unit. 2 side structures, together with 2 end covers, 4 edge structures and 8 corner structures, form the outer shell of a large generator unit.
