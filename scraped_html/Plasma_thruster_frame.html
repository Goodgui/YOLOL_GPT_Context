Plasma thruster frame
Type Thruster device
Function Mounts the nozzle collar of a plasma thruster

Size 288×288×144 cm
Mass 33,507.7 kg
Volume 3,579.88 kv
Corrosion resistance 440
Primary material Kutonium
Composition
Aegisium 25%

Arkanium 5%

Charodium 10%

Kutonium 35%

Vokarium 10%

Ymrium 15%

The plasma thruster frame is a component of the plasma thruster that mounts the superconductors to the engine of the thruster. Four of these are required for a properly assembled plasma thruster, arranged in a 2×2 pattern.
