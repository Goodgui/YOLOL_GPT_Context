Exorium tank end cover

Size 504×504×108 cm
Mass 24,687.87 kg
Volume 2,811.83 kv
Corrosion resistance 560
Primary material Ymrium
Composition
Aegisium 45%

Kutonium 10%

Ymrium 45%

End cover for exorium tank. 2 end covers, together with 2 body halves, complete the shell around the tank. Includes connection point to link with other large generator parts or large module connector.
