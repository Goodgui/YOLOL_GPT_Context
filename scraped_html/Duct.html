Ducts
Type Cargo device
Function Forms resource networks

Ducts form part of a resource network, and can be used to replace cables and pipes. The ducts connect with adjacent and stacked ducts, and with cables and pipes when in contact with them. The dimensions of the ducts are compatable with the grooved part of beams, but they can be used anywhere.
