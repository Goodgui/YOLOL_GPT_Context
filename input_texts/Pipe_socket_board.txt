}}<section begin=summary/>The pipe socket board provides a [[Generator_(Assembly)|generator]] with access to the pipe network. This is an important step in connecting a [[Radiator|radiator]] to the generator. It should be noted that cube [[heatsink]]s attached directly to the generator can also be used for pipe connection.<section end=summary/>

== Basic Usage ==

* Allows for transfer of heat between generator, [[heatsink]]s and [[radiator]]s.
* Can distribute a maximum of 100,000 '''Heat''' per second.
* Connects pipes.
