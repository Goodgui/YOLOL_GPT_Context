}}<section begin=summary/>Tripod weapons are heavy infantry weaponry platforms that can hold different types of tripod weapons. While setting up a tripod takes a moment, tripod weapons can be easily taken off and swapped for another. Tripod weapons operate at a lowered fire rate when not attached to electricity. To attach a tripod weapon to electricity the operating endoskeleton needs to be connected to a resource bridge.<section end=summary/>
