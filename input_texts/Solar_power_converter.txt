}}<section begin=summary/>Solar power converters are devices used to convert the solar power generated by [[Solar panel|solar panels]] into [[Data networks|network]] power, which can be used by other devices or stored in [[Battery|batteries]]. Power converters contain a small internal battery, but only one may be active on a network at any given time. They are an integral part of a solar panel array, as solar power cannot be used directly from the panel.<section end=summary/>
