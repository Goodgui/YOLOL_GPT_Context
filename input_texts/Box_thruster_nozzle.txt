}}<section begin=summary/>The [[Box thruster|box thruster]] nozzle is a device that directs the force generated by the [[Box thruster combustion chamber|combustion chamber]] to produce roughly 20% additional thrust without adding zero extra propellant and electricity cost. Box thrusters do not need nozzles to produce thrust - but it is recommended to have nozzles to increase efficiency per thruster and to protect the combustion chamber in PvP.<section end=summary/>
