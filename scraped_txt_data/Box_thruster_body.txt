}}<section begin=summary/>The [[Box thruster|box thruster]] body is a device designed to house other components required for the box thruster to function, and to provide convenient mounting points along its hull. Unlike [[Triangle thruster|triangle thrusters]], the box thruster body has no interfaces to share propellant and power with adjacent thrusters, and each requires its own [[Hardpoints|hardpoint]] to function. It is also worth noting the device hardpoint on thee thruster body is for the Radiator Base to be attatched without the need of an additional hardpoint.<section end=summary/>
