== Basic information ==

* Core part in any integrated system of multiple [[YOLOL Chip|YOLOL chips]]
* Serves as a physically transportable data storage medium
* Same size and connectivity as YOLOL Chip
** While similar in size and shape, it is visually easy to differentiate from a full chip
* Holds 10 device fields
