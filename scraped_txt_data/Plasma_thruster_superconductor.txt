}}<section begin=summary/>The plasma thruster superconductors are responsible for the magnification of the thrust force generated by the [[Plasma thruster core|core]]. They come in forms with and without struts, and complete superconductor rings (composed of four superconductor parts) can be stacked linearly to add an additional 7,000,000 thrust per ring.<section end=summary/>

== Variants ==

The superconductor parts come in two variants: One with supporting struts, and one without.
