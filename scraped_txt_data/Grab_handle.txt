<section begin=summary/>The grab handle is a device that allows a player to interact with and manipulate an object that is mounted to a [[Hinges|hinge]] or [[Turntable|turntable]], and physically rotate the joint without using [[YOLOL]]. Grab handles work only if a hinge/turntable is unpowered. [[Sliders|Sliders]] cannot be moved with grab handles.<section end=summary/>
